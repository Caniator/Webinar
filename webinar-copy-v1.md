## Speakers

### Andis Cīrulis — VP of Data, Aerones

Andis leads Aerones' data and AI organization, turning years of high-resolution wind turbine inspection data into the predictive models and planning tools used by operators worldwide. He works directly with asset managers to translate operational data into clearer decisions on inspection cadence, repair prioritization, and long-range maintenance budgets.

### Nauris Horsts — Software Product Manager, Aerones

Nauris leads product for Aerones' Customer Portal and Visual Inspection Studio. He partners with wind operators and OEMs to shape the workflows behind fleet inspections, defect tracking, and reporting — making sure every release closes a real gap in how teams move from raw inspection data to maintenance action.
